Pizza

Making a pizza at home requires three main components: dough, sauce, and toppings. The dough is made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. The sauce is typically pureed or crushed tomatoes, and toppings generally feature mozzarella cheese and various meats or vegetables.
